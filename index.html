I am a Software Developer from Northeastern Pennsylvania.

As a child, I was captivated by the world of computers and eagerly explored how to navigate them. The thrill of solving problems and crafting something new always left me in awe. I relished the challenge of mastering computer technology and software. This enduring passion eventually drove me to pursue higher education.

College

My educational journey began at Luzerne County Community College, where I discovered my true passion: software development. During my time there, I not only excelled academically, maintaining a position on the dean's list, but also took on leadership roles such as Vice President of the Computer Information Systems Club. These experiences equipped me with invaluable skills that continue to shape my career. I proudly earned my associate's degree in Computer Information Systems in May 2013.

Prior to completing my associate's degree, I was driven to further my education, leading me to Penn State University. Throughout my time there, I consistently earned a place on the dean's list while expanding my skill set. This experience laid a strong foundation for my career path. In December 2015, I proudly graduated with a Bachelor's Degree in Information Sciences and Technologies.

Career

A few months before completing my degree at Penn State, I embarked on a significant step in my career journey with an internship at TMG Health as a Production Coordinator. In this role, I took on the responsibility of crafting automated claims processing procedures and report automation, marking my entry into the world of professional development. Following a successful probationary period, I was offered a full-time position, allowing me to further cultivate my programming skills and knowledge.

After gaining valuable experience at TMG Health, I seized an exciting opportunity in the field of Web Development with the United States Postal Service (USPS). In 2016, I joined USPS as an Associate Analyst/Programmer, and shortly after, I was promoted to the mid-level position.

During my tenure at USPS, I played a crucial role in the development on a customer service and administrative application for the registration team. I was responsible for its ongoing development, enhancements, and maintenance, gradually assuming full responsibility and ownership of this piece of the application.

As my expertise continued to expand, I was promoted to a senior level position. In this role, I took on various responsibilities, including training new team members, developing new functionalities, and keeping up on continuous learning through online courses to keep up on the technologies we were moving toward. My expereince at USPS provided me with a robust foundation in software development and a deep appreciation for teamwork.

In July 2022, I embarked on a new career journey by joining Learning A-Z, a private company specializing in education technology. This transition marked an exciting new challenge for me, offering opportunities for personal and professional growth within the dynamic field of educational technology.

At Learning A-Z, my role began on application maintenance and transofmation of reporting modules to align with evolving customer needs. These efforts aim to provide customers with enhanced insights into product usage, benefitting both teachers and students alike.

Following my work on the reporting modules, I became a part of a small team responsible for developing new features for the award-winning application, Writing A-Z. This application was honored with the SIIA Codie Award for Best English Language Arts Instructional Solution for Grades PK - 8. The experience on this team has been immensely rewarding, allowing me to contribute to an application that is making a significant impact in education.

My experience at Learning A-Z has been marked by continuous learning and professional development. I've had the privilege of exploring new facets of technology that were not within my purview during my tenure at USPS. This stimulating environment has allowed me to expand my skill set and contribute to innovative solutions in the field of education technology.

Goals

Throughout my journey in Software Development, I have maintained a few key goals:

  • Continuous Learning: My commitment to ongoing learning and skill development extends beyond software development. I thrive on acquiring new knowledge and expertise that not only enhance my abilities in software development but also transcend into other domains.
  • Innovative Problem Solving: I am driven by the challenge of discovering fresh and innovative approaches to problem-solving. This mindset fuels my creativity and empowers me to tackle tasks with a fresh perspective.
  • Contributing to Something Greater: One of my core aspirations is to actively participate in the design and creation of projects that extend beyond my individual efforts. I am deeply motivated to collaborate on initiatives that have a meaningful impact and leave a lasting legacy.
